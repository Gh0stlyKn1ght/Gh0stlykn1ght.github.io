About

Hands-on teaching, no hype.

I’m Gh0stlyKn1ght, a New Jersey-based CS & robotics teacher, FIRST Robotics mentor, and cybersecurity learner. Most days, I’m helping students build things that move, sense, think, and sometimes break in interesting ways. We wire circuits, debug code, run security labs, and ask better questions about how technology actually works.

Outside the classroom, I study autonomous systems security: robotics, drones, embedded systems, and the intersection of AI and safety. I document the journey here — steady, honest, and focused on real progress.

Educator-engineer

Full-year learning paths across robotics, cyber, and engineering; every project maps to real tech careers.

Systems-first

Design reusable components and labs so students can explore safely without losing rigor.

Security-forward

Specialty in web app security and ethical hacking—teaching why systems break and how to build safer ones.

Tools & platforms

Tinkercad, VEXcode, Arduino, Khan Academy, Code.org, digital fabrication, and competition-ready builds.

What I’m aiming for
  • Deliver purposeful experiences, not just features.
  • Keep a tight feedback loop between build → learn → refine.
  • Bridge cyber, robotics, and fabrication into accessible curricula.
  • Mentor young builders, hackers, and creators into confident engineers.
  • Live the mantra: “Smarter, not harder.” Favorite quote: “HACK THE PLANET!”
