Roadmap

Autonomous Systems Security Roadmap

“Smarter not harder. Hack life. Hack the planet.”

Overview

How intelligent machines sense, think, act—and how to secure them.

I’m a robotics educator, cybersecurity practitioner, and FIRST Robotics mentor building an ecosystem around one idea: autonomous systems + safety + security. This roadmap is the blueprint for the next decade of my growth.

Scope

Everything I build—from robotics labs to AI experiments to drone projects to NeonShell—sits under the same umbrella. This document evolves as I evolve.

Pillars

The four pillars of my work

Pillar 01
Robotics

Autonomous Robotics Engineering

Hands-on systems—circuits, sensors, motors, microcontrollers, VEX, FRC. ROS2 basics, HRI, dependable, testable robots.

Pillar 02
AI

Applied AI & Machine Perception

Computer vision, tracking, decision systems, sensor fusion, planning/navigation, LLM reasoning, adversarial ML threats.

Pillar 03
UAS

Unmanned Aerial Systems

Flight controllers, telemetry security, failovers, autonomous navigation, multi-sensor fusion; UAVs as the next testbed.

Pillar 04
Security

Cybersecurity of Autonomous Systems

ROS/ROS2 exploit research, drone telemetry attacks, firmware analysis, web app security, adversarial ML, LIDAR spoofing.

UAV Security
